WE ARE AN ART COOPERATION

THE INNOVATIVE ECCENTRIC ART


Have no office. We work remote from our desks in Hamburg and Berlin. With it’s roots back in 2007 as an agency and re-founded in 2017 by Executive Creative Director Mike John Otto as an open collective of independent minds, most members have more than 20 years of professional practice. We help forward thinking clients to succeed in digital culture by creating culture driven Narrative Experiences and Services that convert humans into brand fans.

Our Collective aims to be intimately associated with iconic visualizations and epic stories, driven by the mixture of pure force of imagination and technical excellence. Epic things often start in the nothing, right from the scratch in what we call – the monkey room. From initial ideas to moodboards, storyboards and prototypes to high-end execution, we offer creative brand consultancy and craft the certain epic look and feel that fits to your brand and products. We are working worldwide for entrepreneurs, agencies and companies in a wide variety of sectors and in the field of creative strategy, film & branded content, services & products, UX & visual design, social experiences & campaign work with an artistic approach.

Our clients work directly and close with us creating a more personal working relationship. The most important question always is how to provide something meaningful or valuable to our clients, their audience and followers. Valuable means a teaching moment, an inspiration, or something entertaining. We call this a Narrative Experience. If you know your brand, know your customers and understand cultural influence, this is the overlapping field to start working and creating unseen concepts. Let’s start a conversation to maybe create your next Narrative Experience.
